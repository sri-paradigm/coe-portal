What is an AI Blueprint?

AI Blueprints are starter kits for AI solutions. A blueprint is a proven solution that has been harvested and refined as a reference architecture to use on other projects.

A blueprint includes decisions, architecture diagrams, and governance-ready artifacts to streamline design reviews and accelerate delivery.

1

Browse Available Blueprints

Review available blueprint descriptions and identify the architecture pattern that best aligns to your project.

2

Reuse and Adapt

Use the blueprint as a starting point for solution design, governance artifacts, implementation approach, and delivery planning.

3

Submit Proven Projects

Contribute new or improved blueprints based on lessons learned, best practices, and successful implementations.
